3. Logo Design System — Atomiq-Inspired ARIA Mark

3.1 Design Concept
The ARIA logo is inspired by the Atomiq logo reference (Lucas Fields, Dribbble) — geometric, sharp-edged, bold letterform reduced to its structural essence. The mark is an abstracted 'A' shape that simultaneously suggests:
	•	A voice signal / transmission peak (fits the voice-first nature of ARIA)
	•	A sharp upward arrow (intelligence, precision, forward-thinking)
	•	A geometric letterform (strong brand recognition — clearly reads as 'A' for ARIA)
	•	A circuit or antenna tip (tech and AI identity)

3.2 Logo Mark Construction

Construction grid: 100 x 100 units

MARK SHAPE (geometric A):
  Left stroke:  from (20, 90) to (50, 10)   — angled left leg
  Right stroke: from (80, 90) to (50, 10)   — angled right leg
  Crossbar gap: negative space between y=52 and y=62 (no bar, just a gap)
  Stroke width: 12 units (thick, confident)
  Corner type:  ALL SHARP — no rounded corners anywhere on the mark
  Terminals:    Flat cut ends (not angled — cut perpendicular to stroke direction)

NEGATIVE SPACE:
  Inner triangle: Removed cutout between the two legs above the crossbar gap
  This creates depth and makes the mark look like a signal transmission tower

SAFE ZONE: 10 units padding on all 4 sides around the mark

  Letter spacing: 0.08em (slightly open)

LOCKUP (Symbol + Wordmark):
  Layout:         Mark LEFT, wordmark RIGHT, vertically centered
  Gap:            Equal to mark width x 0.4

  Layout:         Mark TOP, wordmark BOTTOM, horizontally centered

  Use when:       Square format needed (social media profile, app icon with text)

3.6 Logo Don'ts
	•	Never rotate the mark
	•	Never stretch or squish — always scale proportionally
	•	Never place mark on a patterned or photo background without a solid container
	•	Never add drop shadows to the mark
	•	Never use the gradient variant below 48px — use solid indigo instead
	•	Never place light indigo on white — contrast ratio fails WCAG AA
	•	Never pair the mark with a serif or handwritten font for the wordmark
	•	Never add rounded corners to the geometric mark — it must stay sharp
